Manicotti

Manicotti, also called Manigot by fat italian mobsters, is a delicious stuffed noodle filled with ricota cheese.

Ingredients

  • Manicotti Shells
  • Ricota Cheese
  • garlic
  • Onion
  • Marianra Sauce
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • egg

Steps

  1. Grate the parmessean cheese
  2. Combine the ricotta cheese, the garlic and onion and egg
  3. Cook the shells
  4. Stuff the shells with the cheese mixture
  5. Bake for 40 minutes at 350 F
