Manicotti

Spaghetti and Meatballs is the most classic italian american dish and you have to be an asshole not to love it

Ingredients

  • Spagehtti Noodles
  • Ragu/li>
  • garlic
  • Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • egg

Steps

  1. Boil Water and Cook the Noodles
  2. Combine the ground beef, egg, onion, garlic and parm in a bowl
  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs
  4. Heat the ragu in a pot and throw the meatballs in to cook them for several hours
  5. Once done mix with the noodles. Manga!
