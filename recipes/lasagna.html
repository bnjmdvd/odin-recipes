lasagna

Lasgana is a tasty italian dish made from noodles, mariana sauce, cheese stuffing and a lot of love.

Ingredients

  • Ragu Marinara Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • garlic
  • Onion
  • Ricota Cheese
  • Fresh Mozzerella

Steps

  1. Cook the Noodles
  2. Add the sauce
  3. layer the sauce then the noodles
  4. Add a layer of riccotta cheese and repeat
  5. Top with fresh mozzerella
  6. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes
